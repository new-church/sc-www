"Soundscape and Remembering, A gathering"

On Saturday, 14th September, between 7pm-9pm, guest speaker Rob Evans will be giving a discourse at the Swedenborg Centre with the title “Soundscape and Remembering” focusing on some esoteric aspects of sound and the contemporary ‘soundscape’. Some of artist Michael J Wright's work will be exhibited on that day (Michael's birthday). This is the 1st of a new series of exhibits of Michael's work provided with the blessings of his estate. Enquiries: Rob Evans at [rme142857@gmail.com](mailto:rme142857@gmail.com) or Joe Vandermeer at [joe@swedenborg.com.au](mailto:joe@swedenborg.com.au)

Join in person at the Swedenborg Centre, or on Zoom using [this link](https://us02web.zoom.us/j/89832027177?pwd=L1NDZytIb2MrU0pHMkJ4SVJBdG5EQT09) when the event commences.
